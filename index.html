About Nazaire Electric

Nazaire Electric, LLC is a family owned and operated business that has developed a reputation for quality performance, reasonable rates and fast and friendly service. We are fully licensed and insured with over 20 years of experience in the Commercial, Residential and Industrial sectors. We specialize in troubleshooting, electrical repairs, remodeling, ballast/lamp replacing, conduit and wiring, all types of electrical installations including electrical outlets, highly efficient indoor/outdoor lighting, and new construction wiring in South Carolina and surrounding areas.
